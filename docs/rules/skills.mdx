# Skills & Saves

Heroes use skills to resolve uncertain situations. When you attempt something with a chance of failure, the GM sets a Difficulty Class (DC) and you roll to meet or beat it.

## Skill Checks

Roll the relevant stat die and add your skill bonus. Exceeding the DC succeeds; meeting it is a borderline success at the GM's discretion.

## Saves

Some effects call for a saving throw. The target number is provided by the ability, spell, or trap triggering the save.

## Advantage & Disadvantage

When you have <RuleRef id="advantage" label="Advantage" />, roll an extra die and drop the lowest. With <RuleRef id="disadvantage" label="Disadvantage" />, roll extra and drop the highest.
