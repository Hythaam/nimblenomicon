# Spells

Spellcasting heroes draw on elemental and arcane forces. Spells are organized by **school** (element or discipline) and **tier** (mana cost).

## Spell Tiers

| Tier | Mana Cost | Unlocked At |
|------|-----------|-------------|
| Cantrip | 0 | Level 1 |
| Tier 1 | 1 mana | Level 2 |
| Tier 2 | 2 mana | Level 4 |
| Tier 3 | 3 mana | Level 6 |
| Tier 4 | 4 mana | Level 8 |
| Tier 5 | 5 mana | Level 10 |

<Callout type="tip" title="Upcasting">
  You can spend additional mana (up to your highest unlocked tier) to increase a spell's effect. Only spells with an **Upcast** line benefit from this.
</Callout>

## Spell Schools

- [Fire Spells](/spells/fire) — Damage, status effects, reactive fire defenses
- [Ice Spells](/spells/ice) — Control, slowing, and defensive ice
- [Lightning Spells](/spells/lightning) — Fast area damage and shock effects
- [Radiant Spells](/spells/radiant) — Holy damage and support
- [Necrotic Spells](/spells/necrotic) — Shadow, life drain, and debuffs
- [Wind Spells](/spells/wind) — Movement, slashing, and flying mobility

## Casting Rules

Casting requires a free hand or spellcasting focus, the ability to speak, and enough mana. See <RuleRef id="cast-spell" label="Cast Spell" /> for the full action rules, and <RuleRef id="concentration" /> for spells that require sustained focus.
