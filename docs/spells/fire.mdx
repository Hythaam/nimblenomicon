# Fire Spells

Fire spells deal direct damage and inflict the **Smoldering** minor status, which some Tier 1+ spells exploit for bonus damage.

---

## Flame Dart *(Cantrip)*

**1 Action | Single Target | Range 8**

**Damage:** 1d10. On crit: Smoldering.

**High Levels:** +5 damage every 5 levels.

---

## Heart's Fire *(Cantrip)*

**1 Action | Single Target | Range 4**

**Effect:** Give an ally within Range an extra action. Spend 1 mana to cast this when it is not your turn.

**High Levels:** +1 Range every 5 levels.

---

## Ignite *(Tier 1)*

**2 Actions | Single Target | Range 8**

**Damage:** 4d10 to a <RuleRef id="smoldering" label="Smoldering" /> target, ending the condition on hit.

**Upcast:** +10 damage.

---

## Enchant Weapon *(Tier 1)*

**1 Action | Single Target | Range 4**

**Effect:** Until the start of your next turn, one weapon or natural attack deals +1d10 fire damage on each hit.

**Upcast:** +1d10 damage.

---

## Flame Barrier *(Tier 3)*

**1 Action | Self**

**Reaction:** When attacked, Defend for free. Until the start of your next turn, melee attackers against you take KEY damage (ignoring armor) and gain Smoldering.

**Upcast:** +KEY damage.

<Callout type="note" title="Reaction">
  Flame Barrier is cast as a Reaction — on someone else's turn. You must have at least 3 mana and this spell prepared.
</Callout>
