# Ice Spells

Ice spells emphasize control, movement penalty, and reactive defense. Many inflict <RuleRef id="slowed" />, reducing a target's speed for their next turn.

---

## Ice Lance *(Cantrip)*

**1 Action | Single Target | Range 12**

**Damage:** 1d6 cold or piercing damage. On hit: Slowed.

**High Levels:** +3 damage every 5 levels.

---

## Snowblind *(Cantrip)*

**1 Action | Single Target | Range 8**

**Effect:** Target is <RuleRef id="blinded" /> until the end of their next turn.

**High Levels:** +1 target every 10 levels.

---

## Frost Shield *(Tier 1)*

**1 Action | Self**

**Reaction:** When attacked, gain 2×KEY temp HP and Defend for free. The ice melts and the temp HP are lost at the start of your next turn.

**High Levels:** +2×KEY temp HP.

---

## Shatter *(Tier 2)*

**2 Actions | Single Target | Range 8**

**Damage:** 4d12 cold damage. If the target is <RuleRef id="slowed" />, they are knocked <RuleRef id="prone" /> and the condition ends.

**Upcast:** +1d12 per mana spent.

---

## Cryosleep *(Tier 3)*

**3 Actions | Single Target | Range 8**

**Effect:** Target makes a STR save (DC 14) or is <RuleRef id="incapacitated" /> until the end of their next turn.

**Upcast:** +2 to the save DC per mana spent.
