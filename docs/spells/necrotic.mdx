# Necrotic Spells

Necrotic spells deal shadow and death damage, pull enemies toward you, inflict fear, and drain life. Many necrotic effects are uniquely powerful at low HP — and some are restricted to the **Shadowmancer** class.

---

## Entice *(Cantrip)*

**1 Action | Single Target | Range 8**

**Damage:** 1d4 (ignores armor). On hit: the target is pulled 2 spaces closer to you.

**High Levels:** Die size increments every 5 levels (d4 → d6 → d8 → d10 → d12).

---

## Withering Touch *(Cantrip)*

**1 Action | Single Target | Reach 1**

**Damage:** 1d12. On hit: the target is considered undead for 1 round (making them vulnerable to radiant effects).

**High Levels:** +6 damage every 5 levels.

---

## Shadow Blast *(Cantrip — Shadowmancer only)*

**1 Action | Single Target | Range 8**

**Damage:** 1d12+KEY. Can only be used once per round.

**High Levels:** +1d12 every 5 levels.

---

## Dread Visage *(Tier 2)*

**1 Action | Self**

**Reaction:** When attacked, Defend for free. Melee attackers become <RuleRef id="frightened" /> of you and suffer 1d12 damage if they attack you this round. Costs 2 mana less while <RuleRef id="dying" />.

**Upcast:** +2 damage and +2 effective Armor per mana spent.

<Callout type="warning" title="Dying Discount">
  Dread Visage's mana reduction while Dying makes it a desperate last-resort option. Stay alive long enough to use it.
</Callout>
