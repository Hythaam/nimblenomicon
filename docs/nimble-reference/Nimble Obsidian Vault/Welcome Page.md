**What is this?**
This Obsidian vault contains rules, tools and content for the Nimble TTRPG, intended as a companion for GMs and Players. It is not a replacement for the books/PDFs, and does not contain any of the art, advice text, adventures or worldbuilding.

Under the Nimble licence, it also does not contain unreleased content, such as betas and previews from Patreon. See [[Content Overview]] for a list of all official Nimble content.

For compatibility purposes, the vault does not use any plugins or themes, nor does it use frontmatter in any of the notes. Feel free to use your own, if you like. All notes have also been formatted with the intent of balancing simplicity and readability.

As new content is released, updated or merged, this vault will be updated to reflect that.

---

**How do I use this?**
Feel free to use it however you like for personal use. You might include it in your GM/Worldbuilding vault, or simply use it for quick reference.

The vault also includes templates for monsters, legendary monsters and character sheets.

The [[Vault Index]] contains links to all the most frequently used pages.

---

**Character Sheet?**
This vault includes a [[Character Sheet Template]], which you may use to keep track of characters directly in the vault. While it does not update dynamically, it does take advantage of having all character elements (like class feats, boons, items etc.) easily linkable as individual notes. Simply link to a note using double square brackets.

---

**Homebrew content?**
As a bonus, the vault also includes content from the Nim+ Homebrew zines, featuring a lot of content from many community contributors. You can download the fully illustrated zines for free from [here](https://santuric.itch.io/).
