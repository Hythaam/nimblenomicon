—— CAST ASIDE, BORN ANEW ——

**Hollow One**
Cut off from your patron, you can no longer cast Shadow Blast and you can no longer cast tiered spells using Pilfered Power. However, as a parting token, you have stolen a secret from your patron: The magical Bonescythe, a weapon of sinew and bone, infused with shadowy magic.

**Bonescythe**
Action: Summon a magical Bonescythe, a melee weapon: 2d12 slashing+DEX necrotic damage to each die (Reach: 2). It shatters after you hit with it (or when combat ends). Any Invocations affecting Shadow Blast affect your Bonescythe Instead.

**High Levels.** Every 5 levels, add 1 extra damage die (Level 5: 3d12, Level 10: 4d12, etc.).
