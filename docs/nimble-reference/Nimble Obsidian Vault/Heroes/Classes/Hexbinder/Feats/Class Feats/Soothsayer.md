**Level 5**
Whenever combat begins, roll a Futuresight Die (1d6). Expend it to add or subtract its result from any die a creature within Reach 4 rolls during this encounter.

**Level 10**
Roll 2 Futuresight Dice instead, they have Reach 8.

**Level 14**
Roll your Futuresight Dice with advantage.

**Level 17**
Increase your Futuresight Dice to d8s.

---

> [!tip]- Changing Dice
> As usual, if a Primary Die is reduced to 1, it misses; if increased to its max value, it crits.
