# Oathsworn

**Key Stats:** STR, WIL | **Hit Die:** 1d10 | **Starting HP:** 17 | **Saves:** STR+ / DEX− | **Armor:** All Armor | **Weapons:** STR Weapons

The Oathsworn is a zealous holy warrior who attains great physical and radiant strength through absolute devotion to a higher power. Nothing can sway them from their hunger for justice. They shield allies with divine power, channel righteous judgment when attacked, and mend wounds with magical touch.

## Class Features

### Radiant Judgment *(Level 1)*

Whenever an enemy attacks you and you have no Judgment Dice, roll your Judgment Dice (2d6). On your next melee attack this encounter, if you hit, deal that much additional radiant damage. The dice are expended whether you hit or miss.

### Lay on Hands *(Level 1)*

Gain a magical pool of healing power equal to 5×LVL (recharges on Safe Rest). Action: Touch a target and spend any amount of remaining healing power to restore that many HP.

### Mana & Radiant Spellcasting *(Level 2)*

You know Radiant cantrips and tier 1 Radiant spells. Your mana pool equals WIL + LVL and recharges on Safe Rest.

**Zealot.** Whenever you attack with a melee weapon, you may spend mana (up to your highest unlocked tier) to choose for each mana spent:
- **Condemning Strike.** Deal +5 radiant damage.
- **Blessed Aim.** Decrease target's armor by 1 step for this attack.

**Paragon of Virtue.** Advantage on Influence checks when forthrightly telling the truth; disadvantage when misleading.

### Subclass *(Level 3)*

Commit yourself to an Oath and gain its benefits. Further features unlock at levels 7, 11, and 15.

**Radiant Judgment (2).** Your Judgment Dice become d8s.

**Sacred Decree.** Learn 1 Sacred Decree. Additional decrees unlock at levels 6, 9, 12, 14, and 16.

### My Life, for My Friends *(Level 4)*

You can Interpose for free.

**Tier 2 Spells.** You may cast tier 2 spells.

**Key Stat Increase.** +1 STR or WIL.

### Radiant Judgment (3) *(Level 5)*

Your Judgment Dice become d10s.

**Upgraded Cantrips.** Your cantrips grow stronger.

**Secondary Stat Increase.** +1 DEX or INT.

### Tier 3 Spells *(Level 6)*

You may cast tier 3 spells.

Sacred Decree (2): Learn a 2nd Sacred Decree.

### Master of Radiance *(Level 7)*

Subclass feature. Choose 1 Radiant Utility Spell.

## Sacred Decrees

- **Blinding Aura.** (1/Safe Rest) Action: Enemies in your aura are Blinded until end of their next turn.
- **Courage!** (1/encounter) When you or an ally in your aura would drop to 0 HP, set their HP to 1 instead.
- **Explosive Judgment.** (1/encounter) 2 actions: Expend your Judgment Dice, deal that much radiant damage to all enemies in your aura.
- **Improved Aura.** +2 aura Reach.
- **Radiant Aura.** Action: End any single harmful condition on yourself or another willing creature within your aura. Use WIL times/Safe Rest.
- **Reliable Justice.** Whenever you roll Judgment Dice, roll with advantage (roll one extra, drop lowest).
- **Shining Mandate.** First time each round you are attacked while already having Judgment Dice, select an ally in your aura to roll one and apply it to their next attack. Advantage on checks to see through illusions.
- **Stand Fast, Friends!** When you roll Initiative, grant allies temp HP equal to STR+WIL. You and allies in your aura have advantage against fear and forced movement effects.
- **Unstoppable Protector.** +1 speed. You may Interpose even if restrained, stunned, or incapacitated. If you Interpose for a noncombatant NPC, you may Interpose again this round.
- **Well Armored.** Whenever you Interpose, gain temp HP equal to your STR.
- **Serve Selflessly.** After performing a notable selfless act during a Safe Rest, swap Oathsworn options.

<Callout type="tip" title="Aura of Protection">
Your Sacred Decrees unlock an aura when you choose an Oath subclass at level 3. The aura's reach can be improved with the Improved Aura Sacred Decree.
</Callout>

## Subclasses *(Level 3)*

Choose an Oath that defines your divine mission. Further features unlock at levels 7, 11, and 15.

### Oath of Vengeance

An aggressive avenger who grows more powerful each time evil strikes.

**Level 3 — Aura of Zeal.** When you roll Judgment Dice, roll 1 more. Gain an aura (Reach 4). Your Radiant Judgment also triggers when an ally within your aura is attacked while you have no Judgment Dice.

**Level 7 — Avenger.** When you or an ally within your aura gain any Wounds, set up to that many Judgment Dice to their max. Then move up to half your speed for free.

**Level 11 — Unerring Judgment.** Increase your primary die rolls on melee attacks by 1 whenever you have Judgment Dice.

**Level 15 — Maximum Judgment.** Whenever you are attacked, set a Judgment Die to its max.

### Oath of Refuge

A protective guardian who stands between evil and those under their care.

**Level 3 — Aura of Refuge.** Your shields gain +WIL armor and count as your spellcasting focus. Gain an aura (Reach 4); you can Interpose for an ally anywhere within your aura.

**Level 7 — Face Me, Foul Creature!** When you Interpose, the attacking enemy is also Taunted by you until end of their next turn.

**Level 11 — Glorious Reprieve.** You and allies in your aura cannot drop below 1 HP. Whenever this triggers, they gain 1 Wound instead (heroes still die at max Wounds).

**Level 15 — Divine Grace.** You are resistant to all damage while Interposing.
