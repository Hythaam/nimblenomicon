# Songweaver

**Key Stats:** WIL, INT | **Hit Die:** 1d8 | **Starting HP:** 13 | **Saves:** WIL+ / STR− | **Armor:** Cloth, Leather | **Weapons:** DEX Weapons, Wands

The Songweaver holds a spark granted by a muse at birth — a spark that flows as sharp wit, relentless humor, and moving art. They can bolster allies to legendary feats, make enemies' brains leak out through their ears, and redirect the justified attacks of others to protect their precious moneymaker.

## Class Features

### Wind Spellcasting *(Level 1)*

You know cantrips from the Wind school and 1 other school of your choice. You also know:

**Vicious Mockery.** (Wind cantrip) Action: Range 12. Damage: 1d4+INT psychic (ignoring armor). On hit: target is Taunted during their next turn. High Levels: +2 damage every 5 levels.

### Songweaver's Inspiration *(Level 1)*

(2×WIL times/Safe Rest) Free Reaction: Allow an ally to reroll a single die related to an attack or save (must keep either result).

### Mana & Unlock Tier 1 Spells *(Level 2)*

You unlock tier 1 spells in the schools you know. Mana pool equals (INT×3)+LVL (recharges on Safe Rest).

**Jack of All Trades.** When you Safe Rest, move a skill point as if you just leveled up.

**Song of Rest.** (1/day) When you Field Rest, play a song and allow anyone spending Hit Dice to heal additional HP equal to your WIL.

### Subclass *(Level 3)*

Choose a Songweaver subclass. Further features unlock at levels 7, 11, and 15.

**Quick Wit.** When you roll Initiative, regain 2 spent uses of Songweaver's Inspiration (expire at end of combat).

**Windbag.** Choose 1 Utility Spell from each spell school you know. Additional utility spells unlock at levels 6 and 14 (all at 14).

### Tier 2 Spells *(Level 4)*

You may cast tier 2 spells.

**Key Stat Increase.** +1 WIL or INT.

**Lyrical Weaponry.** Choose 1 ability from the Lyrical Weaponry list. Additional abilities unlock at levels 9, 13, and 17.

### A "People" Person *(Level 5)*

You've met people who will come to your aid. Choose 2 friends you know — summon them via song (1/Safe Rest each):
- **Stompy.** 3 actions: Summon a huge hill giant for 1 round who storms through enemies dealing LVL + Influence check damage to everything in his path.
- **Mal, the Malevolent Imp.** Summon a tiny fiend for 1 night to find dangerous information or "take care" of a problem (Influence check required).
- **Gran Gran (NOT a hag).** Summon for 1 hour to bake WIL+INT pastries that each recover 1 mana, Hit Die, or Wound (expire in 10 minutes).
- **Linos, the Everfriendly.** Summon a legendary flying creature to take your party wherever you need to go.

**Upgraded Cantrips.** Your cantrips grow stronger.

**Secondary Stat Increase.** +1 STR or DEX.

### Tier 3 Spells *(Level 6)*

You may cast tier 3 spells.

Windbag (2): Choose a 2nd Utility Spell from each school.

<Callout type="tip" title="Songweaver's Inspiration and crits">
When you use Inspiration to reroll an ally's attack, they must keep either result — but with the Herald of Courage subclass, they roll with advantage, dramatically improving their odds.
</Callout>

## Lyrical Weaponry

- **Heroic Ballad.** +2 max Inspiration charges. When used to reroll an ally's attack, also grant +WIL damage.
- **Inspiring Anthem.** (1/encounter) Action: Grant all friendly Dying creatures who can hear you 1 HP and 1 action.
- **Not My Beautiful Faaace!** (1/encounter) When you Defend, force attacker to choose another target on failed WIL save (on save: attack you with disadvantage; if they fail by 5+, they attack themselves).
- **Rhapsody of the Normal.** When you roll 4+ on Vicious Mockery, spend an Inspiration charge to suppress all the target's special abilities until end of their next turn.
- **Song of Domination.** (1/encounter) 2 actions: All enemies within 6 spaces who hear you must make a WIL save or be moved up to 6 spaces and can't move on their next turn.
- **Perform!** After performing somewhere inspiring or sparring verbally with a competent wordsmith during a Safe Rest, swap Songweaver options.

## Subclasses *(Level 3)*

Choose a Herald that defines your bardic style. Further features unlock at levels 7, 11, and 15.

### Herald of Snark

A sharp-tongued debuffer who turns enemy mistakes into punishments.

**Level 3 — Opportunistic Snark.** Reaction (when an enemy within Range 12 misses an attack): Cast Vicious Mockery at them for double damage.

**Level 7 — Fight Picker.** (1/turn) When an enemy is damaged by your Vicious Mockery, have one ally Taunt them until end of the enemy's turn instead.

**Level 11 — Chord of Chaos.** (1/encounter) Action: Move ALL creatures within hearing of your song up to 3 spaces in any direction (not into obviously dangerous places).

**Level 15 — Words Like Swords.** Your Vicious Mockery damage becomes 1d6+INT+WIL.

### Herald of Courage

An inspiring presence who elevates allies to legendary heroism.

**Level 3 — Inspiring Presence.** Whenever you use Songweaver's Inspiration, allies within 12 spaces who can hear you gain WIL temp HP.

**Level 7 — Unfailing Courage.** Your Songweaver's Inspiration allows your target to roll with advantage.

**Level 11 — Fire in my Bones.** Your Songweaver's Inspiration also grants your target 1 additional action.

**Level 15 — Chorus of Champions.** (1/encounter) Free Reaction: Give all party members 1 action.
