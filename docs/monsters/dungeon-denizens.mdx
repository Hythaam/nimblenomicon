# Dungeon Denizens

> For some creatures, **YOU** are the loot at the end of the dungeon.

Dungeon denizens lurk in the deepest, darkest corners — waiting in ambush, dissolving treasure, or imitating the very objects adventurers reach for.

---

## Stirges

Blood-drinking vermin that latch on and don't let go.

**Latched On.** You move where your target moves until either dies. Your attacks can't miss or be Defended/Interposed against. Attacks that miss you damage your target instead.

**Evasive Flier.** Attacks against stirges are made with disadvantage.

## Oozes

Amorphous, acidic blobs that split when struck.

**Goopy.** When crit or dealt any slashing damage: summon X ooze minions (size: d6); their attacks inflict Digested.

**Digestive Touch.** Contact with an ooze inflicts the *Digested* condition: they deal an additional X damage for each time the target has been Digested this encounter.

## Mimics

Masters of disguise that pose as mundane objects before striking.

**Sticky.** Mimic hits also Grapple (<RuleRef id="grappled" />) and can Grapple any number of creatures. When crit: release 1 creature (attacker's choice).

**Ambusher.** Mimics always start first and heroes roll Initiative with <RuleRef id="disadvantage" />.

## Dungeon Denizen Loot

Tarnished coins (partially dissolved by acid), ancient bones with traces of gnaw marks, indigestible items (bones, gems, magical trinkets), a leather-bound journal (water-damaged pages), lockpicks, a treasure map (only half), boots (suspiciously untouched by corrosion).
