# Snakemen

*Aggressive hissing noises.*

Lithe and venomous, Snakemen strike hard and wrap their prey tight. Their melee attacks are a constant threat — one bad roll and you're in their coils.

**Shared Trait — Coiling Strike.** On a melee critical hit: the target is Grappled (escape DC 10).
