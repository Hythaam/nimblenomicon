## Sample Encounters

| Party Level | Encounter Options |
|-------------|-------------------|
| 1 | 4 Minions / 2 Bandits |
| 2 | 3 Bandits / 1 Bruiser / 1 Hunter |
| 3 | 2 Bruisers + 2 Bandits / 1 Assassin + 3 Bandits |
| 4 | 1 Captain + 2 Hunters / 1 Mage + 2 Bruisers |
| 5 | 1 Captain + 1 Mage + 2 Assassins |

<Callout type="tip" title="Bandit Tactics">
  Hunters stay at range. Assassins open invisible. The Captain and Bruisers hold the front line. Eliminate the Mage first — Arc Lightning bounces.
</Callout>

## Bandit Loot

A **very** valuable item (stolen; its owner may come looking for it, or reward you for its return), kidnapped person, leather armor, chipped blades, old food, fine art or clothing, a wagon load of some commodity (salt, nails, wool, etc.), a coded letter from a secretive client.
