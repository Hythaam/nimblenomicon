# Kobolds

Small, maniacal dragonlings. Fiercely protective of their own — and absolutely furious when you hurt one of them.

**Shared Trait — Nooooo!** When an ally within 2 spaces dies, this creature attacks once for free.

## Sample Encounters

These encounter suggestions are per hero. For an easier encounter, use the row 1–2 levels lower. For a harder encounter, use 1–2 levels higher.

| Party Level | Encounter Options |
|-------------|-------------------|
| 1 | 3 Kobold Flunkies / 4 Minions |
| 2 | 6 Kobolds / 4 Sneaks / 1 Clanger + 1 Trapper |
| 3 | 2 Clangers + 1 Trapper / 3 Trappers / 6 Sneaks |
| 4 | 4 Trappers / 2 Denwardens + 4 Sneaks |

<Callout type="warning" title="Numbers Matter">
  Even very weak monsters can be far more deadly than intended when they outnumber the heroes by more than 4 to 1. Between 1 and 4 monsters per hero is ideal.
</Callout>

## Kobold Loot

Honey, **lots** of twine, sandwiches (stolen), shiny objects, a dragon painting (poorly—yet lovingly—made), rotting meats, a variety of small traps (cages, spikes, snapping mechanisms).
