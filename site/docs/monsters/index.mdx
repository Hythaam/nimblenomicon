# Monsters

The GM controls monsters during combat. Monsters do not use Heroic Actions or Reactions — they move and use the actions listed on their stat block, then their turn ends. Monsters die when they reach 0 HP.

## Monster Armor

| Armor Type | Rules |
|-----------|-------|
| **None** | Takes full damage including modifiers |
| **Medium (M)** | Ignores all damage modifiers; takes dice total only |
| **Heavy (H)** | Ignores damage modifiers; takes half the dice total (round up) |

<Callout type="tip" title="Tell Your Players">
  Monster armor shouldn't be a secret. Point out the shield, the plate armor, or the iron shell. Use it as a reason to call out the <RuleRef id="assess" label="Assess" /> action.
</Callout>

## Critical Hits vs. Monsters

Hero critical hits **ignore monster armor entirely**. This makes crits especially valuable against armored targets.

## Default Monster Stats

Unless otherwise stated:
- **Size:** Medium
- **Armor:** None
- **Speed:** 6
- **Attack Reach:** 1
- **Saves:** 1d20 + any listed bonuses
