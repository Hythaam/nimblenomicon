# Radiant Spells

Radiant spells channel divine light — dealing armor-ignoring damage to enemies (especially effective against undead) and healing or buffing allies.

---

## Rebuke *(Cantrip)*

**1 Action | Single Target | Reach 4**

**Damage:** 1d6 (ignores armor), does not miss. Deals double damage against undead or cowardly targets (those who are <RuleRef id="frightened" /> or behind cover).

**High Levels:** +2 damage every 5 levels.

---

## True Strike *(Cantrip)*

**1 Action | Single Target | Reach 2**

**Effect:** Give a creature <RuleRef id="advantage" label="Advantage" /> on the next attack they make (until the end of their next turn).

**High Levels:** +1 Reach every 5 levels.

---

## Heal *(Tier 1)*

**1 Action | Single Target+ | Reach 1**

**Effect:** Heal a creature 1d6+KEY HP.

**Upcast:** Choose one per additional mana: +1 target, +4 Reach, or +1d6 healing. If 5+ total mana is spent, you may also remove one negative condition (e.g., <RuleRef id="blinded" />, <RuleRef id="poisoned" />, or 1 Wound).

<Callout type="tip" title="Flexible Healing">
  Heal is one of the most versatile spells in Nimble — each upcast point can extend range, add targets, or increase the healing amount. Spend wisely based on the situation.
</Callout>

---

## Warding Bond *(Tier 1)*

**1 Action | Ally | Reach 2**

**Effect:** Form a bond with an ally. Until the bond ends (concentration), when they take damage, you take half that damage instead.

**Upcast:** +2 Reach per mana spent.

---

## Vengeance *(Tier 2)*

**2 Actions | Self**

**Effect:** Until the start of your next turn, the first creature to damage you suffers KEY radiant damage (ignoring armor) as a reaction.

**Upcast:** +KEY damage per mana spent.
