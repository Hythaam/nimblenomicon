# Lightning Spells

Lightning spells deal fast damage over wide areas and can leave targets **Charged** — a minor status exploited by higher-tier spells.

---

## Zap *(Cantrip)*

**1 Action | Single Target | Range 12**

**Damage:** 2d8. On miss: the lightning fails to find ground and strikes you instead.

**High Levels:** +6 damage every 5 levels.

---

## Overload *(Cantrip)*

**1 Action | AoE | Reach 2**

**Damage:** 2d8 to all others within Reach.

**High Levels:** +4 damage every 5 levels.

<Callout type="tip" title="Charged">
  Whenever a creature takes lightning damage, it becomes **Charged** for 1 minute — a minor status that some spells exploit for extra damage.
</Callout>

---

## Arc Lightning *(Tier 1)*

**2 Actions | Single Target | Range 12**

**Damage:** 3d8. Lightning chains to one additional target within 4 spaces of the first target for half damage.

**Upcast:** +1 chain target per mana spent.

---

## Alacrity *(Tier 2)*

**1 Action | Self or Ally | Range 4**

**Effect:** The target gains +2 Speed and advantage on DEX saves until the end of their next turn.

**Upcast:** +1 target per mana spent.

---

## Stormlash *(Tier 2)*

**2 Actions | Single Target | Range 12**

**Damage:** 4d10. If the target is Charged, they are also knocked <RuleRef id="prone" />.

**Upcast:** +1d10 per mana spent.
