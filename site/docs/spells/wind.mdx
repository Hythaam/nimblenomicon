# Wind Spells

Wind spells aid in moving friends and foes alike around the battlefield. They can also slice and cut through entire groups of enemies and deal vicious extra damage on critical hits.

<Callout type="note" title="Songweaver Exclusive">
  **Vicious Mockery** is a Wind-adjacent cantrip exclusive to the Songweaver class. It is not listed here as a general Wind spell.
</Callout>

---

## Razor Wind *(Cantrip)*

**1 Action | Single Target | Range 12**

**Damage:** 1d4 slashing (<RuleRef id="vicious" label="Vicious" /> — roll 1 additional die on crit damage). Also damages up to 1 adjacent target.

**High Levels:** +2 damage every 5 levels.

---

## Breath of Life *(Cantrip)*

**1 Action | Single Target | Range 6**

**Effect:** Restore 1 HP to a <RuleRef id="dying" label="Dying" /> creature.

**High Levels:** +2 Range every 5 levels.

---

## Blustery Gale *(Tier 1)*

**2 Actions | Single Target | Range 12**

**Damage:** 3d4 bludgeoning. Advantage against flying, Small, or Tiny targets.

**On hit:** Move a Medium target 2 spaces away; Small/Tiny creatures move twice as far; Large creatures move half as far (round down). For each die of forced movement from this spell, deal +5 damage instead.

**Upcast:** +1 movement distance.

---

## Barrier of Wind *(Tier 2)*

**1 Action | Self**

**Reaction:** When attacked at Range. Defend for free. Ranged attacks have <RuleRef id="disadvantage" label="disadvantage" /> against you this round (including the triggering attack).

**Upcast:** +3 Armor.

---

## Fly *(Tier 3)*

**1 Action | Single Target+ | <RuleRef id="concentration" label="Concentration" />: Up to 10 minutes**

**Effect:** Touch a creature and grant it a flying speed of 12.

**Upcast:** +1 additional target.

---

## Eye of the Storm *(Tier 4)*

**2 Actions | AoE | Reach 3**

**Damage:** 4d4+10 bludgeoning to all enemies within Reach.

**Effect:** You may place surviving creatures anywhere within 1 space of the storm's edge on a failed STR save.

**Upcast:** +1 Reach.

---

## Updraft *(Tier 5)*

**3 Actions | AoE | Reach 12**

**Effect:** Enemies within a 5×5 area must repeat a DEX save until they succeed. For each time they failed the save, they suffer 1d6 falling damage and land <RuleRef id="prone" label="Prone" />.

**Upcast:** +2 Range, +1 area size.

---

## Thousand Cuts *(Tier 6)*

**3 Actions | AoE | Range 12**

**Damage:** d44 slashing (rolled with advantage), also damaging enemies within Reach 1 of your target.

**Upcast:** +1 Reach.

<Callout type="tip" title="Rolling a d44">
  Roll 3d4 and drop the lowest die. The leftmost remaining die is the tens place, the second is the ones (e.g., rolls of 2, 3, and 4 deal **34** damage).
</Callout>

---

## Boisterous Winds *(Tier 7)*

**2 Actions | Multi-target | <RuleRef id="concentration" label="Concentration" />: Up to 1 minute**

**Effect:** You and up to 12 allies within Reach 12 gain:
- Ranged attacks have disadvantage against you.
- A flying speed of 12.
- Can move for free once per round.

**Upcast:** +1 minute duration or +2 targets.
