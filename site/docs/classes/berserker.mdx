# Berserker

**Key Stats:** STR, DEX | **Hit Die:** 1d12 | **Starting HP:** 20 | **Saves:** STR+ / INT− | **Armor:** None | **Weapons:** All STR Weapons

The Berserker is destruction incarnate — an unstoppable force of wrath and ruin who grows stronger the longer a fight goes on. Fueled by battle-lust and an unending Rage, they deal ever-increasing damage, laugh in the face of death, and build a Savage Arsenal of brutal combat tricks.

## Class Features

### Rage *(Level 1)*

(1/turn) Action: Roll a Fury Die (1d4) and set it aside. Add it to every STR attack you make. You can have a max of KEY Fury Dice; they are lost when your Rage ends.

**That All You Got?!** When you are attacked, expend 1 or more Fury Dice to reduce the damage taken by STR+DEX for each die spent.

**Your Rage Ends…** If you leave combat, drop to 0 HP, or go 1 round without attacking or Raging.

*Yes! You can Rage and gain another Fury Die even if you are already Raging. If already at max, roll as normal and decide which ones to keep. Your Fury Dice are dice when calculating damage for monster armor.*

### Intensifying Fury *(Level 2)*

If you are Raging at the beginning of your turn, roll 1 Fury Die for free.

**One with the Ancients.** (1/Safe Rest) When faced with a decision about which direction or course of action to take, you can call upon your ancestors to guide you toward the most dangerous or challenging path.

### Subclass *(Level 3)*

Choose a Berserker subclass. Further features unlock at levels 7, 11, and 15.

**Bloodlust.** Expend 1 or more Fury Dice on your turn, move DEX spaces per die spent for free.

### Enduring Rage *(Level 4)*

While Dying, you Rage automatically for free at the beginning of your turn, have a max of 2 actions instead of 1, and ignore the STR saves to make attacks.

**Key Stat Increase.** +1 STR or DEX.

**Savage Arsenal.** Choose 1 ability from the Savage Arsenal.

### Rage (2) *(Level 5)*

Whenever you Rage, gain 2 Fury Dice instead.

**Secondary Stat Increase.** +1 INT or WIL.

### Savage Arsenal (2) *(Level 6)*

Choose a 2nd Savage Arsenal ability.

**Intensifying Fury (2).** Your Fury Dice are now d6s.

## Savage Arsenal

Choose abilities as you level up. You can reassign them after a notable act of destruction or feat of strength during a Safe Rest (Wrath & Ruin).

- **Death Blow.** After dealing damage from a crit, expend any number of Fury Dice; sum the dice and deal double that amount of damage.
- **Deathless Rage.** (1/turn) While Dying, suffer 1 Wound to gain 1 action.
- **Eager for Battle.** Advantage on Initiative. Move 2×DEX spaces for free on your first turn each encounter.
- **Into the Fray.** Action: Leap up to 2×DEX spaces toward an enemy. If you land adjacent to 2+ enemies, make a free attack against 1 of them.
- **Mighty Endurance.** You can survive an additional 4 Wounds before death.
- **MORE BLOOD!** Whenever an enemy crits you, gain 1 Fury Die.
- **Rampage.** (1/turn) After you land a hit, treat your next attack this turn as if you rolled that same amount instead of rolling.
- **Swift Fury.** Whenever you gain Fury Dice, move up to DEX spaces for free, ignoring difficult terrain.
- **Thunderous Steps.** After moving at least 4 spaces while Raging, deal STR Bludgeoning damage to all adjacent creatures where you stop.
- **Unstoppable Force.** While Dying and Raging, taking damage causes 1 Wound (instead of 2) and critical hits inflict 2 Wounds (instead of 3).
- **Whirlwind.** 2 actions: Attack ALL targets within your melee weapon's reach.
- **You're Next!** Action: While Raging, make a Might skill check to demoralize an enemy within Reach 12 (DC: their current HP). On success, they immediately flee the battle.
- **Wrath & Ruin.** After a notable act of destruction or feat of strength during a Safe Rest, choose different Berserker options.

## Subclasses *(Level 3)*

Choose a subclass that shapes your berserker path. Further subclass features unlock at levels 7, 11, and 15.

### Path of the Red Mist

A blood-frenzied killer who grows more devastating with every kill.

**Level 3 — Blood Frenzy.** (1/turn) While Raging, whenever you crit or kill an enemy, change 1 Fury Die to the maximum. **Savage Awareness.** Advantage on Perception checks to notice or track down blood. Blindsight 2 while Raging: ignore the Blinded condition and see through darkness and Invisibility within that range.

**Level 7 — Unstoppable Brutality.** While Raging, gain 1 Wound to reroll any attack or save.

**Level 11 — Opportunistic Frenzy.** While Raging, make opportunity attacks without disadvantage; make them whenever an enemy enters your melee weapon's reach.

**Level 15 — Onslaught.** While Raging, +2 speed. (1/round) You may move for free.

### Path of the Mountainheart

An unbreakable wall who absorbs punishment and keeps fighting.

**Level 3 — Stone's Resilience.** Whenever you expend Fury Dice to reduce incoming damage, add the value of the die to the amount reduced. **Mountainous Tenacity.** Whenever you expend Hit Dice to recover HP, for every 10 HP you would recover, you may heal 1 Wound instead.

**Level 7 — Unbreakable.** (1/encounter) While Raging, if you would suffer your last Wound or other negative condition of your choice, you don't.

**Level 11 — Titan's Fury.** After you miss an attack or are crit by an enemy, Rage for free.

**Level 15 — Mountain's Endurance.** While Dying, if an attack would be a crit against you, it is rerolled instead (when-crit abilities such as Titan's Fury still trigger).
