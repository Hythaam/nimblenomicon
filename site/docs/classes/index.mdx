# Classes

Nimble features a diverse roster of hero classes, each with a unique playstyle and power fantasy.

| Class | Summary | Subclasses | Complexity |
|-------|---------|------------|------------|
| [Berserker](./berserker) | An unstoppable force of wrath and ruin. | Path of the Red Mist; Path of the Mountainheart | ♦ ♦ |
| [The Cheat](./cheat) | A sneaky, backstabbing, dirty-fighting rogue. | Silent Blade; Scoundrel | ♦ |
| [Commander](./commander) | A battlefield tactician, leader, and weapon master. | Vanguard; Bulwark | ♦ ♦ |
| [Hunter](./hunter) | A resourceful survivalist, bowmaster, and skilled tracker. | Shadowpath; Wild Heart | ♦ ♦ |
| [Mage](./mage) | Wield and shape the elements of fire, ice, and lightning. | Chaos; Control | ♦ ♦ ♦ |
| [Oathsworn](./oathsworn) | A faithful guardian, protector, and avenger of the weak. | Oath of Vengeance; Oath of Refuge | ♦ ♦ |
| [Shadowmancer](./shadowmancer) | Summon hordes of expendable shadow minions. | Pact of the Red Dragon; Pact of the Abyssal Depths | ♦ ♦ ♦ |
| [Shepherd](./shepherd) | Master life and death while leading a faithful companion. | Luminary of Mercy; Luminary of Malice | ♦ ♦ |
| [Songweaver](./songweaver) | Inspiring presence, sharp wit, sharper tongue. | Herald of Snark; Herald of Courage | ♦ ♦ ♦ |
| [Stormshifter](./stormshifter) | Master beast and nature. | Circle of Fang & Claw; Circle of Storm & Sky | ♦ ♦ ♦ |
| [Zephyr](./zephyr) | A disciplined martial artist with swift hands and swift feet. | Way of Flame; Way of Pain | ♦ ♦ |

Choose a class that fits how you want to engage with combat and exploration. ♦ = simpler; ♦ ♦ ♦ = most complex.
