# Shadowmancer

**Key Stats:** INT, DEX | **Hit Die:** 1d8 | **Starting HP:** 13 | **Saves:** INT+ / WIL− | **Armor:** Cloth Armor | **Weapons:** Blades, Wands

The Shadowmancer forges pacts with ancient, unfathomable entities — drawing upon incomprehensible power to summon hordes of expendable shadow minions. Why study magic the hard way when you can scheme your way to power from ancient horrors who don't guard it nearly as well as you'd think?

## Class Features

### Conduit of Shadow *(Level 1)*

Your Patron grants you two cantrips:

**Shadow Blast.** (Necrotic cantrip) Action: (1/turn) Range 8. Damage: 1d12+KEY. High Levels: +1d12 every 5 levels.

**Summon Shadows.** (Necrotic cantrip) High Levels: +1 Reach every 5 levels.
- Action: Summon a shadow minion within Reach 1 (max of INT or LVL minions, whichever is lower).
- Action: (1/turn) Command ALL minions to move 6 then attack (Reach 1, 1d12 each).

**Shadow Minions.** Your minions have 1 HP, no damage bonus, and do not crit. They abandon you outside of combat. You and your minions count as different creatures — you can attack once and command them to attack without suffering the Rushed Attack penalty.

<Callout type="tip" title="Minion math">
Because you and your minions are separate creatures, a horde of shadows scales dramatically. Each minion rolling 1d12 can collectively deal massive damage before your patron even notices.
</Callout>

### Master of Darkness *(Level 2)*

Your Patron grants you knowledge of Necrotic cantrips and tier 1 spells.

**Pilfered Power.** Steal power from your patron to cast tiered spells, always at the highest tier you have unlocked. You can do this DEX times before your patron takes notice. Each time you exceed this limit, your patron damages you for half your max HP. This limit resets when you commune with your patron during a Safe Rest.

### THE PACT IS SEALED *(Level 3)*

Choose a subclass and 1 Lesser Shadow Invocation. Further subclass features unlock at levels 7, 11, and 15.

### A Gift from the Master *(Level 4)*

Choose 1 Greater Shadow Invocation. Additional invocations unlock at levels 6, 9, 14, and 18.

**Key Stat Increase.** +1 INT or DEX.

### Tier 2 Spells *(Level 5)*

You may cast tier 2 spells; all spells are cast at this tier.

**Upgraded Cantrips.** Your cantrips grow stronger.

**Secondary Stat Increase.** +1 STR or WIL.

### Shadowmastery *(Level 6)*

Choose 1 Necrotic Utility Spell. Additional utility spells unlock at levels 8 and 14 (all at 14).

A Gift from the Master (2): Choose a 2nd Greater Shadow Invocation.

## Lesser Invocations

- **Abhorrent Speech.** Communicate with horrible creatures (aberrations, undead, etc.).
- **Beguiling Influence.** (1/day) Reroll an Influence check.
- **Blood Sight.** (1/day) Reroll an Examination check. Detect traces of blood on surfaces.
- **Devoted Acolyte.** Learn 2 of: Celestial, Draconic, Deep Speak, Infernal, or Primordial. Advantage on related Lore checks.
- **Eldritch Sense.** Sense the presence of shapechangers or magically concealed creatures within 6 spaces.
- **Gaze of Two Minds.** Touch a willing creature and perceive through its senses while concentrating.
- **Knowledge from Beyond.** Suffer 1 Wound to succeed on a failed Insight or Arcana check.
- **My Favored Pet.** One shadow minion can begrudgingly tolerate you outside of combat for menial tasks.
- **Voice of the Dark.** Communicate telepathically with a humanoid within 6 spaces.
- **Whispers of the Grave.** (1/day) Ask a dead creature 3 yes/no questions.

## Greater Invocations

- **Armor of Shadows.** Reduce all damage by an amount equal to the number of minions you have.
- **Fiendish Boon.** +1 DEX or INT. You have 1 fewer maximum Hit Dice.
- **Hungering Shadows.** When a shadow would crit, your next tiered spell costs no use of Pilfered Power.
- **One with Shadows.** Action: In dim light or darkness, become Invisible until you move or attack.
- **Repelling Blast.** When you hit a Medium or smaller creature with Shadow Blast, push them up to 2 spaces.
- **Shadow Magus.** Your minions gain +4 Reach and deal d10 damage instead.
- **Shadow Spear.** Shadow Blast targets twice as far, ignores cover, and attacks Prone targets with advantage.
- **Shadow Rush.** When your minions attack, have any of them deal max damage instead of rolling, then die.
- **Shadow Warp.** Action: Switch places with a creature within 12 spaces dealt necrotic damage this turn.
- **Swarming Shadows.** When a shadow would crit, summon another shadow adjacent to the target.
- **Vengeful Blast.** Reaction (when a minion dies): Cast Shadow Blast for free.
- **Supplicate.** After communing with your Patron on a Safe Rest, beg to swap Shadowmancer options.

## Subclasses *(Level 3)*

Choose a Pact that empowers your shadow minions. Further features unlock at levels 7, 11, and 15.

### Pact of the Red Dragon

Fire-infused shadow minions in service to a draconic patron.

**Level 3 — Draconic Crimson Rite.** Your Patron grants you knowledge of Fire spells. Your shadow minions become flaming dragon wyrmling shadows. Shadow Blast and minions can deal fire or necrotic damage and inflict Smoldering whenever they crit.

**Level 7 — We'll ALL Burn!** Cast Pyroclasm without Pilfering Power by including yourself in the damage (you have advantage on the save). Choose 1 Fire Utility Spell.

**Level 11 — Heart of Burning Fire.** Regain 1 use of Pilfered Power each time you roll Initiative (expires at end of combat).

**Level 15 — Enveloped by the Master.** Gain 1d4 Wounds to cast Dragonform.

### Pact of the Abyssal Depths

Cold shadow minions from the deep — void, frost, and nightfrost incarnate.

**Level 3 — Master of Nightfrost.** Your Patron grants you knowledge of Ice spells. Gain the ability to breathe underwater. Your shadow minions become beings of nightfrost. Shadow Blast and minions can deal cold or necrotic damage, and whenever they crit, you gain INT+LVL temp HP.

**Level 7 — Shadowfrost.** Your Shadow Blast also Slows targets. Cast Cryosleep or Rimeblades without Pilfering Power by expending 10 temp HP. Choose 1 Ice Utility Spell.

**Level 11 — Glacial Resilience.** (1/Safe Rest) Reaction (when attacked or would gain a condition): Gain 10×LVL temp HP and end ALL negative conditions. Remaining temp HP are lost at end of next turn.

**Level 15 — Cryomancer's Reprisal.** Pay half your max HP to cast ANY Ice spell. The next creature that hits you in melee this encounter takes cold damage equal to half the HP spent.
