# Shepherd

**Key Stats:** WIL, STR | **Hit Die:** 1d10 | **Starting HP:** 17 | **Saves:** WIL+ / DEX− | **Armor:** Mail Armor, Shields | **Weapons:** STR Weapons, Wands

The Shepherd embodies the forces of Life and Death, wielding radiant and necrotic power in balance. They are never alone — a faithful Lifebinding Spirit companion stands beside them in battle, healing the fallen or smiting the wicked on command.

## Class Features

### Keeper of Life & Death *(Level 1)*

You know Radiant and Necrotic cantrips.

**Searing Light.** (WIL times/Safe Rest) Action: Heal or inflict grievous injuries:
- Heal WIL d8 HP to a Dying creature within Reach 6. **OR**
- Inflict WIL d8 radiant damage to an undead or Bloodied enemy within Reach 6.

### Mana & Unlock Tier 1 Spells *(Level 2)*

You unlock tier 1 Radiant and Necrotic spells and gain a mana pool equal to (WIL×3)+LVL (recharges on Safe Rest).

**Lifebinding Spirit.** (Radiant Spell, Tier 1)
- Action: Summon a spirit companion that follows you and is immune to harm. It lasts until you recast it, Safe Rest, or it heals a number of times equal to the mana spent summoning it.
- Action: It attacks or heals a creature within Reach 4 for 1d6+WIL radiant damage (ignoring armor), or heals for the same amount.

Upcasting: Increment die size by 1 (max d12), +1 healing use.

<Callout type="tip" title="Your companion and Rushed Attacks">
You and your companion use the same action pool but count as different creatures — you can each attack once per round without Rushed Attack penalties. Teamwork!
</Callout>

### Subclass *(Level 3)*

Choose a Shepherd subclass. Further features unlock at levels 7, 11, and 15.

**Master of Twilight.** Choose 1 Necrotic and 1 Radiant Utility Spell.

### Tier 2 Spells *(Level 4)*

You may cast tier 2 spells.

**Key Stat Increase.** +1 WIL or STR.

### Sacred Grace *(Level 5)*

Choose 2 Sacred Graces from the list below. Additional graces unlock at levels 9 and 13.

**Upgraded Cantrips.** Your cantrips grow stronger.

**Secondary Stat Increase.** +1 INT or DEX.

### Tier 3 Spells *(Level 6)*

You may cast tier 3 spells.

Master of Twilight (2): Choose a 2nd Necrotic and Radiant Utility Spell.

## Sacred Graces

- **Assist Me, My Friend!** When you make your first melee attack each round, add your Lifebinding Spirit's damage to it.
- **Empowered Companion.** When you spend mana to summon your Spirit, treat it as if you spent 1 additional mana. Maximum die size is now d20.
- **Guiding Spirit.** When your Spirit rolls 6+ on its damage die, the target glows — next attack against them has advantage.
- **Hasty Companion.** +4 Reach for your Lifebinding Spirit. It can also act for free when summoned.
- **Illuminate Soul.** Action: A creature within 6 spaces glows for 1 round — attacks against them gain advantage or disadvantage (your choice). WIL times/Safe Rest.
- **Light Bearer.** Regain 1 use of Searing Light when you roll Initiative (expires if unspent at end of combat).
- **Not Beyond MY Reach.** Target creatures dead less than 1 round for healing. Every 10 HP healed may recover 1 Wound instead.
- **Vengeful Spirit.** Action: Sacrifice your Spirit to transform it into a swirling vortex of radiant light that damages all enemies within 3 spaces of you (ignoring armor) for a number of rounds equal to its remaining healing charges.
- **Serve.** After spending a day tending to a sacred place during a Safe Rest, swap Shepherd options.

## Subclasses *(Level 3)*

Choose a Luminary that defines whether life or death tips the scales. Further features unlock at levels 7, 11, and 15.

### Luminary of Mercy

A healer of miraculous power who channels life into the fallen.

**Level 3 — Merciful Healing.** When an effect caused by you heals a Dying creature, they are healed for twice as much. (1/round) Your Lifebinding Spirit can act for free while you are Dying. **Life is Beautiful.** Harmless creatures such as butterflies are attracted to you; flowers bloom more vibrantly in your presence.

**Level 7 — Conduit of Light.** When an effect caused by you heals HP, expend 1 use of Searing Light to also heal (or damage, ignoring armor) another target within 6 spaces for the same amount.

**Level 11 — Powerful Healer.** (WIL times/Safe Rest) When rolling dice to heal, deal the max possible amount, or give that many temp HP instead.

**Level 15 — Empowered Conduit.** Your Conduit of Light may target 1 additional creature. Regain 1 Searing Light charge when you roll Initiative (expires if unspent at end of combat).

### Luminary of Malice

An offensive spirit wielder who punishes evil with righteous destruction.

**Level 3 — Soul Reaper.** When you use Searing Light to harm an enemy, make a 2nd enemy within range take the same amount of damage (ignoring armor). **Harbinger of Decay.** Vibrant colors and smells are suppressed near you. Foods spoil faster. Your Lifebinding Spirit can shift to a deathly form (zombie dog, devious imp, etc.) and deal necrotic damage.

**Level 7 — Veilwalker's Blessing.** (1/Safe Rest) Reaction (when you would drop to 0 HP): Drop to 1 HP instead and force an enemy within 6 spaces to make a STR save. On failure, they become Bloodied (or drop to 0 HP if already Bloodied).

**Level 11 — Deathbringer's Touch.** Your first melee attack each round against a Bloodied creature is an automatic critical hit. Your Lifebinding Spirit deals additional damage equal to your STR.

**Level 15 — Conduit of Death.** Your Veilwalker's Blessing recharges when you roll Initiative (lost if unspent at end of combat).
