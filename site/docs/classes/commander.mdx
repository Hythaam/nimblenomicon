# Commander

**Key Stats:** STR, INT | **Hit Die:** 1d10 | **Starting HP:** 17 | **Saves:** STR+ / DEX− | **Armor:** Mail Armor, Shields | **Weapons:** All Martial Weapons

The Commander is among the most skilled soldiers in the world — a master of every weapon and form of close combat. A small cohort led by a trained Commander is more feared than innumerable legions without one. They excel at tactical commands, weapon mastery, and strategic leadership that turns the tide of any battle.

## Class Features

### Coordinated Strike! *(Level 1)*

Gain the **Coordinated Strike!** Commander's Order.

**Coordinated Strike!** (1/round) Free action: you and an ally within 6 spaces both immediately make a weapon attack or cast a cantrip for free. You can do this INT times/Safe Rest.

<Callout type="tip" title="Coordinated Strike and Rushed Attacks">
Attacks made via Coordinated Strike do not count against your normal action economy — you and your ally each get a free attack. At level 5, these attacks also ignore disadvantage.
</Callout>

### Commander's Orders *(Level 2)*

Choose 2 Commander's Orders from the list below.

**Field Medic.** Roll 1 additional die for any health potion you administer. When you or an ally spends Hit Dice to recover HP, if you spent at least ten minutes examining their wounds, they add your Examination bonus to the HP recovered.

### Subclass *(Level 3)*

Choose a Commander subclass. Further subclass features unlock at levels 7, 11, and 15.

### Fit for Any Battlefield *(Level 4)*

Choose a Combat Tactic. When you roll Initiative, gain STR Combat Dice (each a d6). (1/attack) Expend a Combat Die to perform a special maneuver. Combat Dice are lost when combat ends.

**Key Stat Increase.** +1 STR or INT.

### Master Commander *(Level 5)*

When you roll Initiative, regain 1 spent use of Coordinated Strike (lost if not spent during that encounter). Attacks made from your Coordinated Strikes also now ignore disadvantage.

**Combat Tactics.** Your Combat Dice are now d8s.

**Secondary Stat Increase.** +1 DEX or WIL.

### Weapon Mastery *(Level 6)*

You may sheathe a weapon and draw a different one 2×/round for free. Choose a weapon type to specialize in.

Fit for Any Battlefield (2): Choose another Combat Ability or gain +1 max Combat Dice.

## Commander's Orders

- **Coordinated Strike!** (1/round) Free action: you and an ally within 6 spaces make a free attack or cantrip. INT times/Safe Rest.
- **Face Me!** Reaction (after an ally within 12 spaces is crit): Taunt that enemy until you drop to 0 HP.
- **Hold the Line!** (1/encounter) Reaction (when an ally drops to 0 HP): Set their HP to 3× your LVL.
- **I Can Do This ALL DAY!** (1/encounter) Reaction (when you would drop to 0 HP): Expend any number of Hit Dice and set your HP to the sum rolled.
- **Move it! Move it!** When you roll Initiative, give yourself and an ally advantage and +3 speed for 1 round.
- **Reposition!** Action/Reaction (on an ally's turn): Command 1 ally to move up to their speed (or 2 allies up to half their speed) for free.

## Combat Tactics

(1/attack) Expend a Combat Die to add one of the following effects:

- **Commanding Presence.** Action: Shout a ≤2-word command. On failed WIL save (DC 10+STR), target obeys for their next turn (if not obviously harmful). Immune for 1 day after.
- **Heavy Strike.** Push a Medium creature STR spaces and deal extra damage equal to a roll of your Combat Die.
- **Inerrant Strike.** Reroll a missed attack, add 1 to the Primary Die, and deal bonus damage equal to a Combat Die roll.
- **Lunging Strike.** +1 Reach; deal extra damage equal to 2× a Combat Die roll.
- **Sweeping Strike.** 2 actions: Damage ALL targets in a contiguous area within reach. Does not miss on a 1.
- **Slashing.** Attacks with slashing weapons cannot miss unarmored enemies.
- **Bludgeoning.** When primary die rolls 7+ with a bludgeoning weapon, ignore Heavy Armor.
- **Piercing.** Attacks with piercing weapons ignore Medium Armor.
- **Rigorous Training.** After training with your party during a Safe Rest, swap Commander options.

## Subclasses *(Level 3)*

Choose a subclass that defines your command style. Further features unlock at levels 7, 11, and 15.

### Champion of the Vanguard

A front-line leader who charges into battle and inspires allies to follow.

**Level 3 — Advance!** (1/round) After moving toward an enemy, gain advantage on the first melee attack. When you use Coordinated Strike, you and all allies within 12 spaces can first move up to half their speed for free.

**Level 7 — Experienced Commander.** Your Coordinated Strike may target 1 additional ally. Gain +1 use of Coordinated Strike/Safe Rest.

**Level 11 — Survey the Battlefield.** When you roll Initiative, regain 1 use of Coordinated Strike. +1 max Combat Dice.

**Level 15 — As One!** Attacks made with your Coordinated Strike also grant advantage and ignore all disadvantage. Chosen allies gain 1 additional action to use on their next turn.

### Champion of the Bulwark

A defensive protector who weathers any assault and guards their allies.

**Level 3 — Armor Master.** You are proficient with plate armor. **Shield Expert.** While wearing a shield, you may Defend 2× each round. The first time each round you block all damage from an attack, make an opportunity attack against the attacker for free.

**Level 7 — Juggernaut.** When you use Coordinated Strike, deal extra damage equal to your armor and add 1 to your primary die.

**Level 11 — Taunting Strike.** (1/turn) You may Taunt a creature you hit until the end of their next turn.

**Level 15 — Shield Wall.** Allies within 2 spaces gain ALL the benefits of the shield you have equipped.
