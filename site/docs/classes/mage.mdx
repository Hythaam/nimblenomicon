# Mage

**Key Stats:** INT, WIL | **Hit Die:** 1d6 | **Starting HP:** 10 | **Saves:** INT+ / STR− | **Armor:** Cloth Armor | **Weapons:** Blades, Staves, Wands

The Mage wields authority over the elements — fire, ice, and lightning — through rigorous scholarly pursuit. Fragile but devastatingly powerful, they customize their spells with Spellshaper techniques and grow into an Archmage capable of reshaping reality itself.

## Class Features

### Elemental Spellcasting *(Level 1)*

You know Fire, Ice, and Lightning cantrips. Use INT for spellcasting rolls.

<Callout type="tip" title="Cantrips vs. Spells">
Cantrips cost 0 mana and scale with your level. Full spells require mana equal to their tier. See <RuleRef id="mana" label="Mana" /> and <RuleRef id="cast-spell" label="Casting Spells" /> for the full rules.
</Callout>

### Mana & Unlock Tier 1 Spells *(Level 2)*

You unlock tier 1 Fire, Ice, and Lightning spells and gain a mana pool. Your mana pool maximum always equals (INT×3)+LVL and recharges on a Safe Rest.

**Talented Researcher.** Gain advantage on Arcana or Lore checks when you have access to a large amount of books and time to study them.

### Elemental Mastery *(Level 3)*

Subclass. Choose a Mage subclass. Further features unlock at levels 7, 11, and 15.

Also learn the Utility Spells from 1 spell school you know. Additional schools unlock at levels 6 and 14.

### Spellshaper *(Level 4)*

You gain the ability to enhance your spells with powerful effects by spending additional mana. Choose 2 Spellshaper abilities. Additional abilities unlock at levels 9 and 13.

**Tier 2 Spells.** You may cast tier 2 spells.

**Key Stat Increase.** +1 INT or WIL.

### Elemental Surge *(Level 5)*

When you roll Initiative, regain WIL mana (expires at end of combat if unused).

**Upgraded Cantrips.** Your cantrips grow stronger.

**Secondary Stat Increase.** +1 STR or DEX.

### Tier 3 Spells *(Level 6)*

You may cast tier 3 spells.

Elemental Mastery (2): Learn the Utility Spells from a 2nd spell school you know.

## Spellshaper Abilities

Use 1 per turn. Gain abilities as you level up.

- **Dimensional Compression.** (1+ mana) +4 range to a spell per additional mana spent.
- **Echo Casting.** (2×mana, min 1) When you cast a tiered single-target spell, cast a free copy on a 2nd target.
- **Elemental Destruction.** (1+ mana) After hitting with a spell, spend up to WIL mana to reroll 1 die per mana spent.
- **Elemental Transmutation.** (1 mana) Change a spell's damage type to Fire, Ice, Lightning, Necrotic, or Radiant.
- **Extra-Dimensional Vision.** (2 mana) Ignore the line of sight requirement of a spell; it phases through barriers to a target you know of within range.
- **Methodical Spellweaver.** (−2 mana) Spend 1 additional action to reduce mana cost by 2 (min 1).
- **Precise Casting.** (1+ mana) Choose 1 creature per mana spent to be unaffected by a spell you cast.
- **Stretch Time.** (2 mana) Reduce the action cost of a spell by 1 (min 1).
- **Study!** After studying arcane books or being tutored by a higher-level Mage during a Safe Rest, swap Mage options.

## Subclasses *(Level 3)*

Your subclass defines your approach to magical control or chaos.

### Chaos

Harness the fraying edges of the mana tapestry for overwhelming power.

**Control Table.** Gain the following powerful reactions (each use of the table may trigger one):
- **I INSIST.** Cast a cantrip for free, ignoring all disadvantage; it cannot miss.
- **ELEMENTAL AFFLICTION.** A creature of your choice within 12 spaces gains the Charged, Smoldering, or Slowed condition.
- **NO.** Choose a creature; it cannot harm a creature of your choice during its next turn.
- **LOSE CONTROL.** Do ALL of the above, but the GM chooses each time.

*Subclass features at levels 7, 11, and 15 are detailed in the Chaos subclass progression.*

### Control

Bend the tapestry of mana to your precise will.

*Subclass features at levels 7, 11, and 15 are detailed in the Control subclass progression.*
