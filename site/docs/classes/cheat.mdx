# The Cheat

**Key Stats:** DEX, INT | **Hit Die:** 1d6 | **Starting HP:** 10 | **Saves:** DEX+ / WIL− | **Armor:** Leather Armor | **Weapons:** DEX Weapons

The Cheat is a street-smart survivalist who believes honor is a luxury only the powerful can afford. Masters of stealth, trickery, and backstabbing, they break every rule to their advantage — changing dice rolls, vanishing into shadows, and landing devastating critical strikes when foes least expect it.

## Class Features

### Sneak Attack *(Level 1)*

(1/turn) When you crit, deal +1d6 damage. This scales as you level: d8 at level 3, 2d8 at level 7, 2d10 at level 9, 2d12 at level 11, 2d20 at level 15, and 3d20 at level 17.

### Vicious Opportunist *(Level 1)*

(1/turn) When you hit a Distracted target with a melee attack, you may change the Primary Die roll to whatever you like. Changing it to the max value counts as a crit.

A target is **Distracted** if it is adjacent to or Taunted by an ally, or if it cannot see you.

<Callout type="tip" title="Magic? The Cheat has some abilities that look magical">
They might be, but don't have to be. You have your ways — no need to explain how you did that, not even to the GM!
</Callout>

### Cheat *(Level 2)*

You're a well-rounded cheater. Gain the following abilities:
- (1/round) You may either Move or Hide for free.
- (1/day) You may change any skill check to 10+INT.
- If you roll less than 10 on Initiative, you may change it to 10 instead.
- You may gain advantage on skill checks while playing any games, competitions, or placing wagers.

### Subclass *(Level 3)*

Choose a Cheat subclass. Further subclass features unlock at levels 7, 11, and 15.

**Thieves' Cant.** You learn the secret language of rogues and scoundrels.

### Underhanded Ability *(Level 4)*

Choose an Underhanded Ability from the list below. Additional abilities unlock at levels 6, 8, 10, 12, 14, 16, and 18.

**Key Stat Increase.** +1 DEX or INT.

### Twist the Blade *(Level 5)*

Action: Change one of your Sneak Attack dice to whatever you like.

**Quick Read.**
- (1/encounter) Gain advantage on an Assess check.
- (1/day) Gain advantage on an Examination check.

**Secondary Stat Increase.** +1 WIL or STR.

### THAT'S Not What Happened! *(Level 6)*

(1/Safe Rest) Action: After a Distracted enemy attacks you, you may change the Primary Die roll to whatever you like (changing the die to the minimum value counts as a miss).

## Underhanded Abilities

- **"Creative" Accounting.** Steal up to INT actions from your next turn; cannot use 2 turns in a row.
- **Exploit Weakness.** Action: Contested INT check to apply Vicious Opportunist to a non-Distracted target for 1 minute.
- **Feinting Attack.** If you miss for the 2nd time in a single round, change the primary die to any result.
- **How'd YOU get here?!** 2 actions: "Teleport" up to 4 spaces adjacent to a Distracted target and attack. On crit, teleport again.
- **I'm Outta Here!** When an ally within 4 spaces is crit, turn invisible until end of next turn and move up to half speed.
- **Misdirection.** Gain INT armor. Whenever you Defend, you may halve the damage instead.
- **Steal Tempo.** When you land a critical hit for the second time on a turn, your target loses 1 action and you gain 1.
- **Sunder Armor (Medium).** On crit vs. medium armor, sunder it until start of your next turn — all melee attacks ignore that target's armor.
- **Sunder Armor (Heavy).** Req. Sunder Armor (Medium). Extends to heavy armor.
- **Trickshot.** Thrown daggers return to hand; on hit, ricochet to another creature within 2 spaces for half damage.
- **Trade Secrets.** After a night talking shop with roguish types during a Safe Rest, choose different Cheat options.

## Subclasses *(Level 3)*

Choose a subclass that defines your roguish specialty. Further features unlock at levels 7, 11, and 15.

### Tools of the Silent Blade

An assassin who strikes from the shadows and leaves no witnesses.

**Level 3 — Amidst All This Commotion…** If a creature dies while you Sneak Attack them, turn Invisible until you attack again or the beginning of your next turn. **Leave No Trace.** Advantage on Stealth checks when at full health.

**Level 7 — Cunning Strike.** (2/encounter) When you land a Sneak Attack, force a STR save (DC 10+INT). On failure, deal maximum Sneak Attack damage instead of rolling (on save, regain 1 use).

**Level 11 — Professional Skulker.** Gain a climbing speed and advantage on all Stealth checks (replaces Leave No Trace).

**Level 15 — KILL.** When you crit an enemy with fewer max HP than you, it dies instantly.

### Tools of the Scoundrel

A manipulator who fights dirty and talks their way out of anything.

**Level 3 — Low Blow.** When you Sneak Attack, spend 2 additional actions to Incapacitate your target on a failed STR save (DC 10+INT). They are Taunted by you regardless of save result. **Sweet Talk.** Gain advantage on Influence checks with NPCs you've just met (until you fail a check or meet a 2nd time).

**Level 7 — Pocket Sand.** (2/encounter) When you Defend against a melee attack, Blind the attacker until start of their next turn and force them to reroll the attack.

**Level 11 — Escape Plan.** (1/Safe Rest) When you would drop to 0 HP or gain a Wound, you don't. Instead, turn Invisible for 1 minute or until you attack.

**Level 15 — Heads I Win, Tails You Lose.** (1/encounter) Attacks this round don't miss, you crit on 1 less than normal, and gain LVL temp HP.
