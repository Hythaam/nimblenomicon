# Hunter

**Key Stats:** DEX, WIL | **Hit Die:** 1d8 | **Starting HP:** 13 | **Saves:** DEX+ / INT− | **Armor:** Leather Armor | **Weapons:** DEX Weapons

The Hunter is a resourceful survivalist, bowmaster, and skilled tracker who calls the wild home. They mark their prey and relentlessly pursue it, harnessing the Thrill of the Hunt to unleash devastating precision attacks — whether raining arrows from distance or closing in for a lethal melee takedown.

## Class Features

### Hunter's Mark *(Level 1)*

Action: A creature you can see is marked as your quarry for 1 day (or until you mark another). It cannot hide from you, and your attacks against it gain your choice of **advantage OR +LVL damage** (choose before each attack).

### Forager *(Level 1)*

Gain advantage on skill checks to find food and water in the wild.

### Thrill of the Hunt *(Level 2)*

Choose 2 Thrill of the Hunt (TotH) abilities. Gain a charge whenever:
- Your quarry dies.
- You hit your quarry in melee or crit your quarry at range.

Each TotH ability costs 1 charge and cannot miss. Abilities that spend charges cannot generate new ones. Unused charges are lost when combat ends.

**Roll & Strike.** Action: If you have no TotH charges, move up to your speed toward your quarry. If you end adjacent to them, make a free melee attack.

<Callout type="tip" title="Thrill of the Hunt charges">
You gain charges by engaging your quarry up-close or landing ranged crits. Managing your quarry choice is key — switching it resets all progress.
</Callout>

### Subclass *(Level 3)*

Choose a Hunter subclass. Further features unlock at levels 7, 11, and 15.

**Tracker's Intuition.** Study tracks and environmental clues to determine the kind and amount of creatures, their direction, key actions, and passage of time at a past encounter.

### Thrill of the Hunt (2) *(Level 4)*

Choose a 3rd TotH ability.

**Key Stat Increase.** +1 DEX or WIL.

**Explorer of the Wilds.** +2 speed; gain a climbing speed.

### Hunter's Resolve *(Level 5)*

Whenever you have no TotH charges, gain Hunter's Resolve until end of your turn: treat all creatures as your quarry for movement and melee attacks.

**Final Takedown.** Action: Spend 1 TotH charge to make a melee attack against your Bloodied quarry. Turn it into a crit and double your Hunter's Mark damage. If they survive, they crit you back.

**Secondary Stat Increase.** +1 STR or INT.

### Versatile Bowmaster *(Level 6)*

When attacking with a Longbow, you may roll 2d4 instead of 1d8; or with a Crossbow, 2d8 instead of 4d4.

Thrill of the Hunt (3): Choose a 4th TotH ability.

## Thrill of the Hunt Abilities

- **Addling Arrow.** Action: Attack with ranged weapon; target's next attack must be against the closest creature chosen at random.
- **Come Get Some!** Action: Attack a target; it is Taunted by you until end of their next turn.
- **Decoy.** When you Defend: the attack misses instead, and you move up to half your speed away.
- **Fleet Feet.** Move up to your speed for free, ignoring difficult terrain.
- **Grease Trap.** (1/encounter) Reaction: Target falls Prone, is vulnerable to fire, and is treated as Smoldering.
- **Hail of Arrows.** (Half range) 2 actions: Shoot all creatures in a 3×3 area; speed halved until end of their next turn.
- **Heavy Shot.** (Half range) Action: Ranged attack; push target (small: 4 spaces, medium: 2, large: 1).
- **Incendiary Shot.** (Half range) Action: Ranged attack + WIL d8 fire damage.
- **Multishot.** (Half range) Action: Attack quarry with ranged weapon, then select a 2nd target within 2 spaces to take the same damage.
- **Pinning Shot.** Spend 3 actions shooting quarry; they are Restrained until escape (DC 10+WIL).
- **Snare Trap.** (1/encounter) Reaction: Move enemy back 1 space, Restrained until escape (DC 10+WIL).
- **Sharpshooter.** Action: If you haven't moved this turn and quarry is 4+ spaces away, attack for double damage.
- **Vital Shot.** (Half range) Action: If quarry is Hampered, ranged attacks ignore armor; if unarmored, double Hunter's Mark bonus.
- **Wild Instinct.** (1/round) If you have no TotH charges, Assess for free with advantage.
- **Remember the Wild.** After spending a day in the wilderness during a Safe Rest, swap Hunter options.

## Subclasses *(Level 3)*

Choose a subclass that defines your hunter's path. Further features unlock at levels 7, 11, and 15.

### Keeper of the Shadowpath

A stealth-focused assassin who strikes before prey knows you're there.

**Level 3 — Ambusher.** When you roll Initiative, use Hunter's Mark for free. Gain advantage on the first attack each encounter. **Skilled Tracker.** Advantage on checks to track creatures. **Skilled Navigator.** Cannot become lost by nonmagical means.

**Level 7 — Primal Predator.** (1/encounter) Your weapon attacks ignore cover and armor this turn.

**Level 11 — Pack Hunter.** When you mark a creature, also mark another within 6 spaces for free.

**Level 15 — Apex Predator.** Use Primal Predator twice per encounter. Gain 1 TotH charge when you roll Initiative.

### Keeper of the Wild Heart

A resilient survivalist with an unbreakable bond with nature.

**Level 3 — Impressive Form.** +5 max HP. Upgrade Hit Dice to d10s. **I Have the High Ground.** When you roll Initiative or gain TotH charges, move up to half your speed for free, ignoring difficult terrain.

**Level 7 — Resourceful Herbalist.** While Safe Resting near plants/fungi, collect WIL Healing Salves. **Healing Salve.** Action: Heal yourself or an adjacent creature WIL d6 HP (expires on Safe Rest).

**Level 11 — Ha! I'm Over Here!** (1/Safe Rest) If an attack would drop you to 0 HP, instead move up to your speed away and take no damage.

**Level 15 — Unparalleled Survivalist.** Gain +WIL armor. When attacking with a ranged weapon, move half your speed for free first.
