# Stormshifter

**Key Stats:** WIL, DEX | **Hit Die:** 1d8 | **Starting HP:** 13 | **Saves:** WIL+ / STR− | **Armor:** Cloth or Leather Armor | **Weapons:** Staves, Wands

The Stormshifter embodies the union of mortal and nature — wielding lightning and tempests as others wield blade and shield, and shifting into fearsome beasts ranging from friendly to vicious to utterly horrible. They are as one with storm and beast, flexible enough to fill any role the party needs.

## Class Features

### Master of Storms *(Level 1)*

You know cantrips from the Lightning and Wind schools.

**Beastshift.** Action: Transform into a harmless beast (squirrel, pigeon, etc.). While transformed, you can speak with animals. This form lasts until you drop to 0 HP, cast a spell, or end it on your turn for free. You have DEX Beastshift charges; they reset on a Safe Rest.

*Tiny Beasts.* Whenever you shapeshift into a Tiny beast, attacks against you are made with disadvantage, but ANY damage ends that shapeshift form.

### Direbeast Form *(Level 2)*

You can Beastshift into a **Fearsome Beast** (Large): Gain DEX+LVL temp HP, the Gore attack, and Fearsome.
- **Gore.** Action: 1d6+LVL damage; on hit, gain LVL temp HP.
- **Fearsome.** When you Interpose or Defend, spend 1 mana to force the attacker to reroll (you choose either result).

**Mana & Unlock Tier 1 Spells.** You unlock tier 1 Wind and Lightning spells. Mana pool equals (WIL×3)+LVL (recharges on Safe Rest).

### Subclass *(Level 3)*

Choose a Stormshifter subclass. Further features unlock at levels 7, 11, and 15.

Direbeast Form (2): You can Beastshift into a **Beast of the Pack** (Medium): Gain +DEX speed, Supercharge, and the Thunderfang attack.
- **Thunderfang.** Action: 1d4+LVL piercing damage. Whenever you crit or kill enemies, Thunderfang gains cumulative +1d4 lightning damage until combat ends.
- **Supercharge.** Spend up to WIL mana; next Thunderfang deals +1d8 lightning damage per mana spent (you take this damage on a miss).

### Tier 2 Spells *(Level 4)*

You may cast tier 2 spells.

**Key Stat Increase.** +1 WIL or DEX.

**Stormcaller.** Learn a Utility Spell from each spell school you know. Additional utility spells unlock at level 7.

### Direbeast Form (3) *(Level 5)*

You can Beastshift into a **Beast of Nightmares** (Tiny): Gain the Sting attack and Silent But Deadly.
- **Sting.** (1/round) Action: Reach 0. 1d4 piercing + 3×LVL acid damage (ignoring armor); on crit: 4×LVL damage.
- **Silent But Deadly.** Speed: 2. Cannot Defend or Interpose. Attackers cannot target you until you become conspicuous.

**Upgraded Cantrips.** Your cantrips grow stronger.

**Secondary Stat Increase.** +1 STR or INT.

### Chimeric Boon *(Level 6)*

Choose 2 Chimeric Boons. Whenever you shapeshift into a Direbeast form, modify it with 1 Chimeric Boon you know. Additional boons unlock at levels 9, 12, and 17.

**Expert Shifter.** Gain 1 additional use of Beastshift per Safe Rest (additional uses at levels 9 and 12).

**Tier 3 Spells.** You may cast tier 3 spells.

<Callout type="tip" title="Beastshift and spellcasting">
While in Direbeast form, you cannot cast spells unless you take the Circle of Sky & Storm subclass (Creature of the Fey, Level 3). Plan your shifts around combat needs.
</Callout>

## Chimeric Boons

- **Beast of the Sea.** Move, breathe, and fight underwater without penalty.
- **Climber.** Walk across walls and ceilings; ignore difficult terrain.
- **Fleet Footed.** +2 speed. Advantage on Stealth checks and against the Grappled condition.
- **Earthwalker.** +2 armor. Burrow through dirt and unworked rock at half speed. Advantage against Prone.
- **Keen Senses.** Advantage on Perception and Assess checks. Unaffected by Blinded.
- **Leader of the Pack.** Advantage against fear and charm effects for yourself and allies within 6 spaces.
- **Phasebeast.** When shifting between forms, teleport up to 6 spaces to a place you can see.
- **Prehensile Tail.** Creatures you hit in melee your size or smaller are Grappled; against larger creatures, you move with them.
- **Winged.** Gain a flying speed. Forced movement moves you twice as far while flying.
- **Be Wild.** After spending a day with wild animals during a Safe Rest, swap Stormshifter options.

## Subclasses *(Level 3)*

Choose a Circle that defines your relationship with nature. Further features unlock at levels 7, 11, and 15.

### Circle of Fang & Claw

A beast-focused shifter who moves fluidly between dangerous forms.

**Level 3 — Swiftshift.** When you roll Initiative, Beastshift or move for free. While transformed, shift between Direbeast forms for free (or as a reaction by spending 1 mana); however, free shifts don't grant temp HP. **Windborne Protector.** (1/encounter) Reaction: Spend 2 mana to shift into a Fearsome Beast, then Interpose from up to 12 spaces away and Defend for free. **Friend of Beasts.** Beasts won't attack you until you harm them. Transform into harmless beasts without spending a Beastshift charge.

**Level 7 — Unleash the Beast.** (1/encounter) When you miss, crit instead. **Storm Wake.** (1/encounter) Action: Spend 3 mana to shift into a Beast of the Pack, then teleport in a straight line up to 12 spaces, dealing WIL d8 lightning damage to creatures you choose adjacent to your path.

**Level 11 — Master of Forms.** Your shapeshift forms can have 2 Chimeric Boons at a time. **Venomous Gaze.** (1/encounter) Action: Spend 2 mana to shift into a Beast of Nightmares, then entice a creature within 12 spaces to move 2×WIL closer (WIL save with disadvantage, repeat until they save or can't move further). If they end in your space, Sting them for free.

**Level 15 — Master of Forms (2).** 2 additional Beastshift uses per Safe Rest. Choose 2 additional Chimeric Boons. Direbeast forms can have 3 Chimeric Boons at a time.

### Circle of Sky & Storm

A spellcasting druid who harnesses elemental power from afar.

**Level 3 — Deepening Study.** Choose the Ice or Radiant school to learn. **Creature of the Fey.** You may cast spells while Beastshifted. **Attuned to Nature.** (1/day) Add LVL to any skill check related to nature or weather.

**Level 7 — Raging Tempest.** Whenever you crit with a tiered spell, cast a cantrip for free from a different school you know (same level of dis/advantage).

**Level 11 — Primordial Force.** Spending 2+ mana on a spell grants an additional effect:
- Ice: Gain WIL temp HP.
- Lightning: Deal +WIL damage.
- Radiant: Heal a creature within 6 spaces WIL HP.
- Wind: Gain a flying speed this turn; move up to 6 spaces for free.

**Level 15 — Master of Storm.** You can concentrate on 1 lightning spell and 1 wind spell simultaneously. (1/Safe Rest) Cast Ride the Lightning for 0 mana.
