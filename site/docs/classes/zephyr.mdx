# Zephyr

**Key Stats:** DEX, STR | **Hit Die:** 1d8 | **Starting HP:** 13 | **Saves:** DEX+ / INT− | **Armor:** None | **Weapons:** Melee

The Zephyr dedicates their life to the relentless pursuit of kinetic perfection — honing their body into a blur of motion through ceaseless training in remote wilds or mountain monasteries. Speed is both weapon and shield. They harness inner momentum to achieve supernatural agility, executing every motion with flowing precision, unarmored and unstoppable.

## Class Features

### Iron Defense *(Level 1)*

Your armor equals DEX+STR as long as you are unarmored.

### Swift Fists *(Level 1)*

Your unarmed strikes are not subject to disadvantage from Rushed Attacks, and their damage is 1d4+STR.

<Callout type="tip" title="Unarmed and unarmored">
The Zephyr's Iron Defense scales with both DEX and STR, rewarding investment in both key stats. Don't put on armor — it removes this bonus entirely.
</Callout>

### Swift Feet *(Level 2)*

While unarmored, gain +2 speed and +LVL Initiative.

**Burst of Speed.** When you roll Initiative, gain DEX Bursts of Speed for that encounter. (1/turn) Spend 1 Burst of Speed for any of the following maneuvers for free:
- **Slipstream.** Defend, and the attack misses.
- **Whirling Defense.** Defend and apply your armor to every attack this round.
- **Swiftstrike.** Attack on your turn, ignoring disadvantage from Rushed Attacks.
- **Windstep.** Move on your turn, ignoring difficult terrain.

### Subclass *(Level 3)*

Choose a Zephyr subclass. Further features unlock at levels 7, 11, and 15.

**Kinetic Momentum.** Whenever you gain a Wound, gain a Burst of Speed.

**Ethereal Projection.** (1/day) Meditate for 10 minutes to project an ethereal version of yourself up to 30 ft. away, passing through solid objects. You see through its eyes for up to 10 minutes.

### Unyielding Resolve *(Level 4)*

Ignore the first Wound you would suffer each encounter (when-Wounded abilities, such as Kinetic Momentum, still trigger).

**Key Stat Increase.** +1 DEX or STR.

**Martial Master.** Choose a Martial Arts ability. Additional abilities unlock at levels 6, 8, 10, 12, 14, 16, and 18.

### Reverberating Strikes *(Level 5)*

Add LVL bludgeoning damage to all of your melee attacks.

**Secondary Stat Increase.** +1 INT or WIL.

### Infuse Strength *(Level 6)*

Action: Make an unarmed strike against an ally to infuse them with strength instead of harming them. Expend any number of Hit Dice and heal them as you would heal yourself during a Field Rest (roll them and add your STR to each).

Martial Master (2): Choose a 2nd Martial Arts ability.

## Martial Arts Abilities

- **Airshift.** Cannot be Grappled while conscious. While moving, travel across all terrain as normal ground (walls, water, lava, etc.).
- **Blur.** (1/encounter) When you Defend, first move up to half your speed away, taking no damage if now out of range or behind full cover.
- **Bodily Discipline.** Spend 1 action to end any non-Wound condition on yourself.
- **Enduring Soul.** Each time you roll Initiative, gain Hit Dice equal to the actions you get on your first turn (expire at end of combat).
- **I Jump On His Back!** While using Windstep, move into the space of a creature your size or larger to jump on its back. Gain advantage on melee attacks against it; damage you avoid is dealt to it instead.
- **Kinetic Barrage.** Whenever you miss an attack, gain a cumulative +STR bonus to all damage for the rest of the encounter.
- **Mighty Soul.** Cannot be moved against your will. May gain a Wound to add STR to any saving throw result.
- **Quickstrike.** When you Interpose, make a free unarmed strike against the enemy first.
- **Use Momentum.** When you avoid all damage from a melee attack (miss or Defend), swap places with the attacker and redirect the attack to another target within its reach.
- **Vital Rejuvenation.** When you receive healing for the first time on a turn, heal another target within 6 spaces for STR HP.
- **Windstrider.** If you move through a willing creature's space using Windstep, they can move with you to any space adjacent to your path.
- **Focus.** After meditating alone in a windy place during a Safe Rest, swap Zephyr options.

## Subclasses *(Level 3)*

Choose a Way that defines your martial philosophy. Further features unlock at levels 7, 11, and 15.

### Way of Pain

A counterattacker who punishes aggression with shared suffering.

**Level 3 — Bring the Pain.** (1/round) You may turn any melee attack against you into a crit. Whenever you are crit, reduce damage by half; the attacker takes the same amount you took (ignoring armor). Suffer 1 Wound to double the damage the attacker takes.

**Level 7 — Share My Pain.** Your Swiftstrike can also target a 2nd creature within Reach 2.

**Level 11 — Pain Sharpens the Mind.** While Bloodied, gain advantage on the first attack each turn and on all saves.

**Level 15 — Echoed Agony.** Your Swiftstrike can also target a 3rd creature within Reach 4.

### Way of Flame

A blazing warrior who channels inner fire into devastating strikes.

**Level 3 — Exploding Soul.** (1/round) On your turn, you may suffer a Wound. Whenever you gain a Wound, deal STR+Wounds damage to creatures you choose within 2 spaces (ignoring armor) and inflict the Smoldering condition.

**Level 7 — Blazing Speed.** +2 speed while using Windstep. After you cease movement with Windstep, enemies you passed through take STR+DEX fire damage. You may have Smoldering enemies take double damage, ending the condition.

**Level 11 — Chain Reaction.** (1/turn) When you crit, deal fire damage equal to STR+Wounds to creatures of your choice within 2 spaces. Repeat targeting creatures not yet damaged within 2 spaces of any already damaged.

**Level 15 — Burning Soul.** Double any fire damage you deal.
