# Resting & Downtime

Heroes need rest to recover from the rigors of adventure. There are two tiers of rest: **Field Rests** (quick recovery) and **Safe Rests** (full recovery).

---

## Field Rests

<RuleRef id="field-rest" /> — taken while out adventuring to regain HP by spending <RuleRef id="hit-dice" />.

### Catch Breath

Requires at least **10 minutes** to tend to injuries.

- Expend any number of <RuleRef id="hit-dice" /> one at a time.
- **Roll** each Hit Die and **add your STR**.
- Regain that many HP.

### Make Camp

Requires at least **8 hours** with food and sleep.

- Take the **maximum value** for each Hit Die you expend, instead of rolling.
- Add your STR to each Hit Die as usual.

<Callout type="note">
If your STR is negative, subtract it from each HD expended.
</Callout>

---

## Safe Rests

<RuleRef id="safe-rest" /> takes place in a **safe location** designated by the GM — typically an inn, a secret oasis, a well-stocked cabin, or near a sacred shrine. **Camping in the open wilderness or in a dungeon is not sufficient** for Safe Rest benefits.

After a Safe Rest, heroes recover:
- ✅ All HP
- ✅ All Hit Dice
- ✅ All mana (and other class-specific resources)
- ✅ 1 Wound healed

Safe Rests are also an opportunity for **downtime activities**.

<Callout type="tip">
If your table prefers to skip downtime and narrate past extended rest, that's perfectly fine! For a more realistic recovery, consider 1 week per Wound healed.
</Callout>

---

## Lodging

When taking a Safe Rest at an inn or similar establishment, the quality of lodging affects the experience — and may provide **Temporary Boons**.

| Quality | Cost (per person/day) | Benefit |
|---------|----------------------|---------|
| **Poor** | 5 sp | Basic rest; may lead to complications |
| **Comfortable** | 2 gp | Standard Safe Rest benefits |
| **Lavish** | 10 gp | Standard benefits + 1 **Temporary Boon** |

### Temporary Boons (Lavish Lodging)

Roll 1d8 (or GM awards based on spending) for a boon that lasts until the next Safe Rest:

| 1d8 | Temporary Boon |
|-----|----------------|
| 1 | Recover 2 additional Wounds |
| 2 | Gain LVL temporary HP |
| 3 | Gain KEY temporary Hit Dice |
| 4 | +1 Speed |
| 5 | Inspired (reroll any die, once) |
| 6 | Advantage vs. Fear/Charm/similar |
| 7 | Learn an important rumor |
| 8 | +KEY mana |

Boon tiers: **Minor** (~10 gp), **Major** (~100 gp), **Epic** (~1,000 gp). Adjust to fit your campaign.

## Downtime Activities

The time between adventures is called **Downtime**. Not every moment need be roleplayed — much of it can be summarized. Example downtime activities:

| Activity | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| **Retrain** | Change chosen abilities, features, or (story permitting) a subclass |
| **Gather Information** | Meet NPCs, pick up news, collect rumors and job leads |
| **Personal Goals** | Pursue backstory quests or smaller personal objectives |
| **Buy & Sell** | Get new equipment; sell treasure from your adventures |
| **Perform** | Play music, tell stories, compete, or entertain to earn gold or fame |
| **Craft** | Create weapons, armor, or items from acquired materials |
| **Socialize** | Build alliances, make friends — or enemies |
| **Invest** | Use gold to invest in businesses or trade ventures for future profit |
| **Mentor** | Teach a skill or ability to another character or NPC |
| **Research** | Investigate a mystery, study ancient texts, uncover hidden knowledge |
| **Serve** | Aid a patron or deity for a favor, or perform charity |
| **Build** | Establish a home base, start a business, craft siege weapons, or build anything else (GM and setting permitting) |
