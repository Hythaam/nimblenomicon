# Equipment

## Equipment Proficiency

Heroes can use any equipment they like, but each class lists weapons and armor they are **proficient** with.

- **Weapons without proficiency:** Cannot crit.
- **Armor worn without proficiency:** Using the <RuleRef id="defend" /> reaction costs **1 additional action**.

**Swapping Equipment:** A hero can sheathe proficient weapons or shields and equip another for **free, 1/round**.

---

## Armor

Armor represents your hero's ability to dodge or block damage when you use the <RuleRef id="defend" /> reaction. While unarmored (or in regular clothes), your **Armor equals your DEX**.

<Callout type="tip">
**Defend Yourself!** Standing next to an enemy and doing nothing means you *will* get hit. Use the Defend reaction to reduce incoming damage.
</Callout>

### The Defend Reaction

<RuleRef id="defend" /> — Spend 1 action (when it is not your turn) to reduce damage from any single attack by your **Armor value**. Some damage types may not be avoidable at the GM's discretion (e.g., psychic damage, certain areas of effect).

## Weapon Properties

| Property | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| **2-handed** | Can be held in one hand, but must be used in 2 hands to attack |
| **Load** | Requires extra actions to reload before each shot |
| **Reach X** | Target must be within X spaces; if unspecified, Reach 1 (adjacent) |
| **Range X** | Can attack from up to X spaces away |
| **Thrown X** | Treat as a ranged attack up to X spaces; once thrown, you no longer have it |
| **<RuleRef id="vicious" />** | Roll 1 additional die whenever you roll crit damage |
| **Light** | Can be dual wielded; see Dual Wielding |

<Callout type="note">
Only the **Primary Die** (leftmost die) determines whether an attack crits. Roll the Vicious extra die on every crit roll.
</Callout>

### Dual Wielding

Heroes may wield **2 Light weapons** simultaneously. While dual wielding, you may gain <RuleRef id="advantage" /> on one attack with those weapons, **1/round**.

- **STR 3+:** May dual wield 1-handed weapons without the Light property.
- **STR 2:** May dual wield with 1 weapon lacking the Light property.
- **Unarmed:** Fists/feet count as dual wielded.

**Dual wielding different weapons:** Roll dice for both and choose either result (1/round limit still applies).

### Unarmed Strikes

Roll **1d4**; on hit, deal **1 + STR** damage.

### Improvised Weapons

Default to **1d4+STR** or **1d6+STR**. The GM may allow larger dice if the improvised weapon is appropriately large. Improvised weapons may break on a crit.

## Spell Scrolls

Spell scrolls are **single-use** inscribed spells. Casting from a scroll does not cost mana and requires no magical ability — anyone who can read the language it's written in can use it. Reading a scroll aloud takes the same number of actions the spell normally requires and consumes the scroll.

If you do not already know a spell from that school, you must succeed on a **DC 10 Arcana check**; failure wastes the scroll.

| Tier 1 | 35 gp |

## Wands

Wands cast spells without consuming mana and do not require magical ability. Unlike scrolls, **wands can be recharged and reused**. Characters proficient with wands can use one from any spell school spending only the normal actions.

Characters **not proficient** must succeed on a **DC 10 + spell tier Arcana check**. Success or failure, the charge is spent.

## Magical Items

Most magical items cannot be purchased — they are acquired through adventuring. Some specialty shops may carry a very small selection.

| Rarity | Level Range | Typical Cost |
|--------|-------------|--------------|

| **Very Rare** | Levels 8–18 | 5,000–500,000+ gp |
| **Legendary** | Levels 15–20 | 1,000,000+ gp or Priceless |
