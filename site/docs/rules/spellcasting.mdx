# Spellcasting

Heroes with spellcasting classes use **mana** to power their spells. Each spell has a tier equal to its mana cost.

## Cantrips

<RuleRef id="cantrip" label="Cantrips" /> are basic spells that cost 0 mana. Their power scales with character level.

## Casting a Spell

1. Choose the spell to cast.
2. Have one free hand or a spellcasting focus.
3. Spend the required mana.
4. Roll the spell's damage or effect die.

## Concentration

Some powerful spells require <RuleRef id="concentration" />. You can only concentrate on one effect at a time. If you are critically hit, make a DC 10 STR save or lose concentration.

## Upcasting

Spend additional mana (up to your highest unlocked tier) to increase a spell's effect.
