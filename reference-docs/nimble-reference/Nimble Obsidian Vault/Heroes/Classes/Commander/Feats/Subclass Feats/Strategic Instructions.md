Action: Relay an Instruction to an ally that can hear you, granting them certain effects. You can have 1 Instruction active (new Instructions replace active ones). Instructions last until the end of combat or they are replaced. Known Instructions:
* **Get Back In Line!** +2 speed.
* **Slow Their Advance!** Attacks apply Slowed.
* **Get Their Attention!** Attacks Taunt the target until the end of their next turn.
