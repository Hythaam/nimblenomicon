## Keeper of the Knife
When choosing a *Thrill of the Hunt* ability, you may instead choose a *Thrill of the Wild* ability instead.
#### **Level 3**
**Natural Athlete** - Gain advantage on grappling checks and saves
**One with Nature** - Gain advantage on Insight and Naturecraft checks when you Assess your quarry, as well as on skill checks related to natural creatures.
#### **Level 7**
**The Challenge** - When you kill your mark with a melee attack, gain KEY×(Creature Level) temp HP
#### **Level 11**
**Mercy Killing** - Crits against hampered creatures are vicious
#### **Level 15**
**Thrill Seeker** - (KEY/encounter) Gain a Thrill of the Wild charge when you successfully grapple with your quarry.

## Thrill of the Wild
#### Animal Handling
After a creature you are riding makes a move action, add one space of forced movement in the direction of your choice. You may choose to stop riding the creature before this final movement.
#### Chipped Blade
Melee attacks with any edged weapon are Vicious
#### Down in the Dirt
(1/round) Ignore the rushed attack penalty for a melee attack against a creature you are grappling
#### Field Dress
Action: Attack your Hampered quarry with a melee weapon, ignoring their armor or doubling your Hunter's Mark damage bonus if they have none.
#### Relentless
(1/round) If a marked creature moves away from you, move up to half your speed towards them.
#### You're Next
2 actions. Pin a creature using a bladed weapon (losing access to the weapon until it is retrieved). The creature is Restrained until they can escape (DC 10+WIL).
