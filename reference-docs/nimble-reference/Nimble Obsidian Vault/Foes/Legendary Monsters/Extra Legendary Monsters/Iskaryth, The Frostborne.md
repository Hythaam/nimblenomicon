*Level 8 Solo Ice Dragon*

**HP:** 190 | **Armor:** Heavy | **Saves:** STR+, WIL+

> **Rimeblade Aura.** Others suffer 2 damage for each space they move while within Reach 2.

> **Shielded by ice.** Vulnerable to Fire and Bludgeoning damage. Iskaryth is enraged and gains advantage on her next attack.

---

**ACTIONS:** After each hero's turn, Fly 12 OR choose one:
* **Wall of Ice.** (1 use, Reach 10) Summon a wall of ice 5 spaces in a line.
* **Bite.** Move 4. (Reach 2) 2d6+10. On damage, Frostbite: Can't use Reactions (healing or fire damage ends).
* **Shattering Claws.** Move 4. (Reach 2) 3d6. Any die can be chosen as the primary die against a Hampered target. On crit: deal +20 damage instead of rolling additional dice.
* **Blizzard.** (1 use, AoE, Reach 6) 3d6+20 damage. DC 14 STR save or frozen in place (Restrained). Save to escape, or any Fire/Bludgeoning damage.
